Updated September 02, 2015.

The Floradora, or Florodora, is a classic cocktail that has been run into near-obscurity, but you'll still find it in many bartending guides.

It was named after the first Broadway musical hit in the 1900's, which originated in London. In How's Your Drink?, Eric Felten tells a great story surrounding the drink.

Originally, the drink was made with raspberry syrup, but now uses framboise liqueur (raspberry-flavored) almost exclusively. There are a number of raspberry liqueurs available, with Chambord (black raspberry) being one of the most common.

Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 ounces gin
  • 1/2 ounce fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 ounce framboise liqueur
  • Ginger ale
  • Lime wedge for garnish

  • Prep Time: 3 minutes

  • Total Time: 3 minutes
  • Yield: 1 Cocktail
Preparation
  1. Pour the gin, lime juice, and framboise into a highball glass filled with ice.
  2. Top with ginger ale.
  3. Garnish with a lime wedge.
